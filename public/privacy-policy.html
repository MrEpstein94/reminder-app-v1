Reminder app

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Overview

Reminder App is used to manage personal reminder schedules and send reminder emails or text messages to approved recipients.

Information We Collect

  • Names used for reminder profiles.
  • Email addresses and mobile phone numbers entered for reminder delivery.
  • Calendar links and calendar event data required to generate reminder messages.
  • Basic message delivery records needed to operate the reminder service.

How We Use Information

  • To generate daily reminder summaries.
  • To send reminder emails or SMS messages requested by the account owner.
  • To support account setup, delivery troubleshooting, and reminder management.

SMS and Consent Data

Mobile opt-in information, consent, and phone numbers are used only for reminder message delivery. Mobile opt-in data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Sharing of Information

Information is shared only with service providers required to deliver reminders, such as email or messaging platforms. Information is not shared for third-party marketing.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, contact the Reminder App owner at the support contact provided during setup.
