SMS Program Details

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Who Receives Messages

Messages are sent only to the account owner’s personal mobile phone number or to family members who have directly consented to receive reminder notifications.

How Users Opt In

Users opt in during reminder app setup when the account owner enters the recipient’s mobile number and enables text reminders for that person. The account owner is responsible for confirming that each recipient has provided direct consent before messages are activated.

What Messages Contain

  • Daily schedule summaries.
  • Reminder notifications tied to calendar events.
  • Service-related message updates for the reminder program.

Required Disclosures

  • Message frequency varies based on reminder settings and calendar events.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP to opt out.
  • Reply HELP for help.
